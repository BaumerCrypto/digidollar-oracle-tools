⚠️ Tier 1 Reality Check
All devices share the same flat network — your oracle, your phone, your kid's tablet, your smart TV. If any device is compromised, it can see and potentially reach your oracle on the local network. This is why Tier 3 introduces VLAN isolation.

What Tier 1 protects against: internet-facing attacks (port scanning, brute force). The host firewall and port forwarding rules ensure only P2P traffic reaches your oracle from outside.

What Tier 1 does NOT protect against: local network attacks from compromised devices on your WiFi.
